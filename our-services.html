Our Services

Boundary Surveying

Turn to Holton Surveying & Design LLC for your next property survey and let us provide you with timely, accurate information about your land. We operate throughout Sisseton, SD, and surrounding areas, offering boundary surveying services for property owners.

General Surveying

Need to know more about your land? We offer ACSM and ALTA surveys for registering a plat, boundary surveying for determining property lines, and GPS surveys for elevation and topography analysis. Our state-of-the-art technology ensures accurate results and comprehensive documentation.

  • ACSM/ALTA Surveys
  • Boundary Surveys
  • Elevation Certificate Survey
  • GPS Surveys
  • LOMA Surveys
  • Mortgage Surveys
  • Hillside Surveying

Partition Plats

We provide services for property subdivision, boundary assessment, easement evaluation, and more. Whether you need assistance with plat divisions or land dispute consultation, we're here to help.

  • Easements
  • Subdivision Plats
  • Property Line Adjustments
  • Property Division

Land Dispute Consultation and Expert Witness

In legal disputes over land boundaries or property lines, we can serve as expert witnesses, providing credible testimony and consultation expertise for mediations and arbiters. Contact us for land surveying services and expert consultation.
